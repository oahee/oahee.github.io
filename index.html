Details of The Glorious Quran

Even though there are many translations of the meanings of the Quran, they do not help the reader in linking the Arabic words to their meanings. The only purpose of The Glorious Quran Word-for-Word Translation is to facilitate learning the language of the Quran. Even though a few word-for-word translations exist but the format of the present work is different from them.

The usefulness of The Glorious Quran

  • 1. The meaning of each word is given right below it.
  • 2. The translation provided in the left column is kept close to the Arabic and not literal. The objective of the whole exercise is to enable the reader to understand directly from Arabic.
  • 3. Last, but not least, the layout is such that it can also be used for regular recitation enabling constant revision.
